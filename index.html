Profile Summary

Sumaira Irum Malik is a senior Public Health, Immunization, and Vaccine Management Specialist with more than 15 years of international experience supporting Ministries of Health and global partners including UNICEF, WHO, Gavi, and UNFPA.

She specializes in strengthening routine immunization systems, optimizing vaccine supply chains, conducting Effective Vaccine Management (EVM 2.0) assessments, and developing Comprehensive Improvement Plans (CiP). Her work combines technical immunization leadership with digital innovation including Power BI dashboards, DHIS2 integration, remote temperature monitoring systems, GIS risk mapping, and centralized immunization data platforms.
